Olivia Isabel Rodrigo

(born February 20, 2003) is an American singer-songwriter and actress.

  • Early life
    • Childhood residence: Grew up in Temecula, California.
    • Family background: She is an only child. Her father is of Filipino descent, while her mother has German and Irish ancestry.
    • Songwriting inspiration: Country music by Taylor Swift.
  • Achievements
    • Album: Sour was the most-streamed album overall and remains the most-streamed album by a female artist on the platform.
    • Variety: Songwriter (2021), Storyteller (2023).
    • Grammy Awards: Best Pop Solo Performance for "Drivers License," Best New Artist, and Best Pop Vocal Album (2022).
